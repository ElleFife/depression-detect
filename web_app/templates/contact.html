DepressionDetect started while partcipating in Galvanize's Data Science Immersive Program. I worked as an engineer after graduating from the University of Michigan in 2014 and quickly became frustrated with the industry standard tools for data analysis. I started dabbling in Python and quickly fell in love with some of its machine learning capabilities.

ThGalvanize was a means to enhance my data science skillset, with DepressionDetect a culmination of a very intensive 3 months of training. I hope you've enjoyed my exploration as much as I have. Thanks for visiting, Ky

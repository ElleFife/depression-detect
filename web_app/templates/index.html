Your data helps!

Become a data donor.

A major challenge for mental health researchers is access to data. Should you choose to donate, your data will be be used incorporated into a neural network that learns predictors of depression from acoustic features of speech. Your audio recordings are anonymized upon donation. If you have lived with depression, know someone with depression, or if you would just like to help, please consider sharing your data.

Depression Detection is an open-source project rooted in Python. The project seeks to objectively identify early signs of depression from acoustic features of speech. Early detection and treatment of depression is essential in promoting remission, preventing relapse, and reducing the emotional burden of the disease. To date, the model has learned from over 70 people, but it needs your help!
